Description

Malai Kulfi is a traditional Indian frozen dessert known for its rich, creamy texture and intense flavor. Unlike regular ice cream, kulfi is denser and melts more slowly. It is often flavored with cardamom, saffron, or pistachios and served on a stick or in a matka (earthen pot).

Ingredients

  • Full Fat Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Chopped Nuts
  • Saffron Strands
  • Cornflour or Condensed Milk

Steps

  1. Boil the milk
  2. Add sugar, cardamom and saffron
  3. Add chopped nuts
  4. Cool the Mixture
  5. Pour into kulfi molds
  6. Unmold and Serve
