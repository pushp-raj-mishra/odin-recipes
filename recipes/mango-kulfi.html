Mango Kulfi

Home

Description

Mango Kulfi is a seasonal, fruity twist to the classic kulfi, made with ripe mango pulp, thickened milk, and cardamom. It's creamy, tropical, and perfect for summer.

Ingredients

  • Full Fat Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ripe Mango Pulp
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Chopped Nuts
  • Saffron Strands(optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the milk
  2. Add sugar, cardamom and saffron
  3. Add chopped Nuts
  4. Cool the Mixture
  5. Blend Mango Pulp
  6. Mix the Mango Pulp in Milk and Nuts mixture
  7. Pour into kulfi molds
  8. Unmold and Serve
